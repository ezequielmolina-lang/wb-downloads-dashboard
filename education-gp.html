Methodology & contact

Sources. Downloads are read from both World Bank repositories — WB Documents (documentos.bancomundial.org) and the Open Knowledge Repository (openknowledge.worldbank.org) — and summed across every language version of each product.

Window. Products with a document date in 2024–2026. Reader geography is from WB Documents (~99% of its downloads are geo-located; OKR downloads are not). “United States & high-income” is the World Bank's residual region grouping, here almost entirely the United States.

Totals combine WB Documents + OKR. LAC Education appears here as its fully-reconciled total (218,776 / 37 products); on the LAC Human Development comparison it uses the like-for-like figure (169,943 / 29) so it is comparable to Health & Social Protection.
